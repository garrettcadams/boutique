Description

Created by celebrity Australian makeup artist Rosie Jane; this light airy scent blended into a hydrating lotion containing organic ingredients of shea butter, jojoba oil and sunflower seed extract with notes of fig, ember and gardenia, James is earthy and romantic and can be worn by all. 

- Paraben FREE 
- Sulfate FREE 
- Phthalate FREE 
- Phosphate FREE
- Cruelty FREE 
- Made from recycled and recyclable packaging
  
Made in CA

Our Beauty Notes: We are SO into this warm, romantic, perfectly balanced smelling scent bottled into an incredibly hydrating body lotion. 

Use

Using morning and night to keep skin hydrated and smelling refreshed.

Ingredients 

Water, Glycerin, Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea Butter) ((Certified Organic)), Hydrogenated Palm Kernel Glycerides, Cetearyl Olivate, Helianthus Annuus (Sunflower) Seed Oil ((Certified Organic)), Ethylhexyl Palmitate, Dimethicone, Sorbitan Olivate, Fragrance (100% Natural Parfum), Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Oil ((Certified Organic)), Cocos Nucifera (Coconut) Oil ((Certified Organic)), Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Extract ((Certified Organic)), Citrus Grandis (Grapefruit) Fruit Extract ((Certified Organic)), Hydrogenated Palm Glycerides ((Certified Organic)), Cetearyl Alcohol, Ceteareth-20, Coco-Glucoside, Coconut Alcohol, Phenoxyethanol, Caprylyl Glycol, Ethylhexylglycerin, Hexylene Glycol

Tips

Pair with the body wash and perfume for lasting fragrance all day long.
