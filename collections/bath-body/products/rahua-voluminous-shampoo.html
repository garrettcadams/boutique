RAHUA VOLUMINOUS SHAMPOO

Rahua

Description

As Seen In: GOOP & Vogue

Best Natural Shampoo, Glamour Magazine 2016

This 100% natural, organic, responsibly harvested shampoo cleanses to create body and bounce and is safe for color-treated hair. Rahua Voluminous Shampoo’s natural formula safely and effectively cleanses and refreshes hair without drying hair like other volumizing lines. Citric juices break down oils, effectively extending time between washes, resulting in healthier hair. Lavender and eucalyptus provide calming aromatherapy while Ungurahua oil strengthens hair.

Get Voluminous Conditioner HERE

Our Beauty Notes: This delivers some serious volume giving us insanely bouncy, shiny manes. The citrus and and green tea work together to get you clean from build-up from both your hair and scalp, while offering some serious lift. Balancing this with with moisture from quinoa without weighing hair follicles down. Omega-9 works deeply to fill hair follicles weak areas giving strands a true sense of deep health and an overall strength. When paired with the Omega 9 Hair Mask weekly it takes results to the next level. 


Use

 Apply on thoroughly wet hair to begin shampoo and work into a lather. Rinse and follow with Rahua Voluminous Conditioner.

Complementing products: Rahua Voluminous Conditioner and Omega 9 Hair Mask.

RAHUA-UNGURAHUA - fortifies weak, damaged strands while nourishing and regenerating the scalp and hair follicles.

LEMONGRASS & GREEN TEA - create body, volume and bounce by balancing and removing excess oils from the scalp and hair shaft, without compromising the integrity of the hair.

LAVENDER - provides an aromatherapeutic experience.

EUCALYPTUS – well-known for refreshing and clarifying properties, extends time between shampoos.
