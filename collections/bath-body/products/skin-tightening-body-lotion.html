PRTTY (pronounced "Pretty") Skin Tight body lotion is a unique one-of-a-kind product with exceptionally high quality and beneficial ingredients and properties. We love to use this body lotion as a unique makeup-like body lotion that helps minimize imperfections leaving us confident and radiant (even applying it to our faces high-points occasionally as a highlighter). It is a lotion for everyone - women, men, children - all ages, shapes, sizes, and nationalities. Coming in several shades from Plain to Deep Dark these can be customized for your personal needs and preferences. Prtty Peaushun was designed for anyone and everyone. 

It's a skin conditioning, moisturizing, healing and soothing lotion. It is effective on sore muscles, minor wounds, tattoos, sunburn, bug bites, scaly dry skin sensitive skin and more. It even naturally repels insects, evens out blotchy skin tone from sun damage, hormonal conditions, skin conditions and concerns. It's natural conditioning also shows off muscle definition.

Colors Descriptions 

  • Plain is the only peaushun that does not contain mica (natural minerals), therefore it does not offer a sheer coverage. It is a clear lotion with the same healing and conditioning benefits as the shades in creating a healthy, noticable glow. 
  • Light is a golden champagne shade that offers shine and a subtle glow.
  • Medium is a true gold shade that offers shine, a subtle glow and can enhance a tan. 
  • Dark is a bronze shade that offers shine, a subtle glow and can enhance a tan.
  • Deep Dark is a cocoa bronze shade that offers shine, a subtle glow and can enhance a tan.
