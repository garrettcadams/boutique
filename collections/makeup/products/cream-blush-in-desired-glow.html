Cream Blush in Desired Glow

Kjaer Weis

$ 32.00
Add to cart
Description

Brand Seen In: ELLE, Vogue, InStyle, Marie Claire & More

"Kjaer Weis will change how you think about organic makeup" - InStyle Magazine

Ideal for light to medium skin with golden undertones leaving a sunkissed, just back from vacation glow. 

- Blends beautifully on the skin, using either fingers or a brush
- Produced in Italy
- CCPB Certified Organic
- Cruelty Free NOT tested on animals
- Gluten FREE
- This product is not vegan
- Refill Option 
- NO artificial ingredients, such as petroleum, parabens, artificial coloring and fragrance, petrochemical emulsifiers

“Desired Glow gives the illusion of just returning from holiday, with the look of a subtle tan.” – Kirsten Kjaer Weis Makeup Artist & Founder 

Our Beauty Notes: One of our favorite things about this cream blush is it works well on all skin types - all ages. That's the wonderful thing about cream blush. Kjaer Weis, in particular, delivers this to the next level. Pure luxury. 

Use 

The Kjaer Weis Cream Blush goes on beautifully when applied with the fingers, especially gently dabbed on the apple of the cheek. It leaves the wearer with a fresh-faced, “just back from a run” look. The cream blush can also be applied with a large (artificial hair) brush, gently swept over the cheek area.

Ingredients 
Ricinus Communis (Castor) Seed Oil, Caprylic/Capric Triglyceride, Candelilla Cera, Copernicia Cerifera (Carnauba) Wax , Zea Mays (Corn) Starch, Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea) Butter, Beeswax, Rosa Rubiginosa Seed Oil, Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil, Tocopheryl Acetate, Ammonium Glycyrrhizate, Vanilla Planifolia Fruit Extract, Gardenia Florida Fruit Extract. May Contain +/- Mica, (CI 77019), Iron oxide (CI 77491), Iron oxide (CI 77492), Iron oxide (CI 77499), Titanium dioxide (CI 77891), Carmine (CI 75470). Caution – contains Carmine. Certified Organic by the CCPB, Italy (95% of the raw materials included in this cream blush are ORGANIC) Certified Organic: Of the agriculturally derived ingredients in this product, a minimum 95% comes from organic farming. 

Tips 

Blend from the apples of your cheeks out toward your ears in natural light to ensure a streak less finish.
