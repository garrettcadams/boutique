Glow Philtre

KYPRIS

$ 88.00
Add to cart
Description 

Brand Seen In: InStyle & Green Product Company of the Year, 2014

Kypris creates a treatment mask blending refined pomegranate pith enzymes, softening thalassic extracts of sea fennel stem cells & sea algae with ultra hydrating silver ear mushroom to revitalize skin’s surface. Use as a hydrating exfoliation pre-makeup or as a weekly indulgence to maintain clear pores, smooth texture, and a vibrant luminosity.

Benefits 

Gentle, efficient exfoliation *  Smooths skin for  makeup application prep  *  Softens skin * Clears pores * Sloughs outermost layer of skin for better product penetration * Brightens *  Imparts vibrant glow


OUR BEAUTY NOTES: When it comes to sourcing local, wildcrafted, sustainable and clinically effective ingredients Kypris is among the best. So when they introduced masks to their intelligent line-up we literally lept with anticipation - and they did not dissapoint. This masks has us completely swooning and using it weekly before applying makeup. The luminous skin that accompanies after use is beautiful and extra bonus points for exfoliating and keeping breakouts at bay.  

*Kypris may not be used with coupon codes per requested by brand and honored by their global partners due to the highest quality of sourcing.*

Use

Apply a thin layer of Glow Philtre to a clean face and neck avoiding the eye area. Leave on skin for up to 20 minutes, if sensitive up to 5 minutes. Massage as you rinse with a facial mitt or wash cloth. Follow with a gentle cleanse. Use up to three times per week to prep skin for makeup application or to encourage renewal, clear pores, soft texture, and luminosity. Stop use if irritation occurs. For external use only.

Size: 46 mL
 
Ingredients

ACTIVE INGREDIENTS

POMEGRANATEFermented from pomegranate pith, enzymes gently encourage renewal

GARDEN OF SEA VEGETABLESThalasso therapy returns us to our primordial home in the water to revitalize our spirit and to calm, soothe, plump, brighten, and soften skin

SILVER EARA seemingly magical mushroom holds hundreds of times its weight in water to drench & hydrate even the most parched skin 

CERAMIDESLipid molecules nourish, revive, & soothe chapped complexions

NEROLI FLOWER A delicate floral indulgence to soften skin and romance your senses

 ALL INGREDIENTS
