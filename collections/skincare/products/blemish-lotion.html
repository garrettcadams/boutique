Description
Brand Seen In: Cosmopolitan, Vogue, Refinery29, Zoe Report, Today Show, Lucky, Marie Claire, Allure, Style, Glamour, ELLE, Bazaar & More 

"Certified Chic" - Vogue

This talc-free, overnight spot treatment and drying lotion treats acne and blemishes, reducing redness, irritation, and inflammation. 1oz.   

    Our Beauty Notes: No way we're ever without a blemish spot treatment, right? This is a must have for our Beauty Bag and one we use to help banish pesky or hormonal pimples. 

    Indie Lee tugged at our heart strings initially with her story of overcoming a brain tumor to discover the link between conventional beauty and its potential health harm. After learning about her story, she then struck us with a genius product line that conquers disbelieve in clean beauty. Since regaining her health, she has taken the clean beauty world by storm creating the chic, effective and modern (right up our alley!): Indie Lee. 

    Ingredients 

    100 Natural: Purified Water, Isopropyl Alcohol, Colloidal Sulfur, Zinc Oxide, Salicylic Acid, Magnesium, Aluminum Silicate (Clay), Camphor, Iron Oxide

    Use

    Do Not Shake; allow product to settle to the base of the bottle. Dip a cotton swab into the bottom sediment and dab onto affected areas. Allow product to sit overnight before removing with warm water. For external use only.
