Siren Lipstick (+Colors)

Vapour Organic Beauty

$ 25.00
Add to cart
Description 

As Seen In: Lucky, Allure & More 

"Seriously glam." - Lucky Magazine 

Siren is a striking new advance in lipstick formulation. Pure plant ingredients are processed without chemicals to produce a moisturizing shine that rivals the performance of its mainstream counterparts.

Siren is a long wearing lipstick and lip conditioner in one amazing chemical free formula.

- Naturally long wearing
- Superior texture, never sticky or dry
- Palette flatters all skin tones
- Wear Siren alone or layer with Elixir gloss
- Revitalizes and moisturizes with organic emollients and antioxidants
- Proprietary formula moisturizes while allowing skin to breathe
- Revolutionary non-petroleum, chemical-free formula
- No chemicals to ingest by mouth or through the skin

    Our Beauty Notes: We are lipstick and lip gloss obsessed. We can admit that. Our favorite part about these lipsticks (besides being clean and luxe) is they are perfect for day-meets-evening wear colors. Okay, that, and because they toss perfectly in our office desk, car, purse, gym bag, and diaper bag. 

    Use

    Apply directly to lips or by using a lip brush. Layer with Lux Organic Lip Conditioner or Elixir Plumping Lip Gloss for best results. 

    Ingredients 

    Organic Ricinus Communis (Castor) Seed Oil, Organic Cera Alba (Beeswax), Castor Isostearate Beeswax Succinate*, Mica, Organic Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil, Natural Fruit Flavour Oils, Organic Punica Granatum (Pomegranate) Seed Oil, Camelina Sativa Oil, Organic Rosa Damascena (Rose) Buds, Organic Melissa Officinalis (Lemon Balm) Leaf Extract, Wildcrafted Commiphora Myrrha (Myrrh) Gum, Stevia Rebaudiana (Stevia) Leaf Extract [+/-: Titanium Dioxide (CI 77891), Iron Oxides (CI 77491, 77492, 77499), Carmine (CI 75470), Manganese Violet (CI 77742)]

    * Eco-cert

    Net weight: 0.11oz/3.11g

    Siren is made with 70% organic ingredients and 30% natural mineral pigments. Siren is designed to protect lips from moisture loss and environmental assault. Siren’s botanical infusion contains nourishing Myrrh, calming Rose Buds, restorative Lemon Balm, antioxidant Pomegranate, hydrating Jojoba Oil and essential fatty acid-rich Camelina Oil.
