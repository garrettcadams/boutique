Review

Canonical URL: http://schema.org/Review

Thing > CreativeWork > Review

A review of an item - for example, of a restaurant, movie, or store.

Usage: Between 250,000 and 500,000 domains
  • Leave public feedback on this term 💬
  • Check for open issues.
  • 'Review' is mentioned in layer: core
  • 'Review' is mentioned in extension layer: pending
